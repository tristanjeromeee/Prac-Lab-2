Membership Details

Regular Membership - This type of membership, enables the user to recieve notification for the manga that they bookmarked, they will also be able to view the status of the mangas that they bookmarked. Since, this type of membership is free, there are lesser functions that can be performed in this type of account.

Premium Membership - This type of membership, enables the user to recieve notifications, have earlier release date for the manga that they bookmarked, they will also be able to view the status of the mangas that they bookmarked, they will also be excempt from any form of advertisements, and lastly they will also be able to make request on mangas that they want to be translated. This type of account, will be collecting a monthly fee of 500 pesos.
